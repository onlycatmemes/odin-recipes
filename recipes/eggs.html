Eggs

A complete guide on how to make fried eggs

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Margarine or Butter
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Heat a skillet on high
  2. Add margarine or butter to skillet
  3. Add eggs after butter has melted and skillet is hot
  4. Break yolk if desired
  5. Cook until egg whites are mostly firm
  6. Flip and cook for approx 30 seconds to a minute
  7. Plate, and season with salt and/or pepper
